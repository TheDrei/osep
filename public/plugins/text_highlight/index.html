The goal of Magwayen Organics is to contribute to  protecting marine biodiversity by getting consumers to switch to more sustainable personal care products. Given that our efforts are meant to help reduce chemical and plastic pollution that harm marine ecosystems, we intend to develop the following marine-safe products:
  • Reef-safe sunscreen
  • Plastic-free shampoo and conditioner bars
We will be improving on the existing Magwai marine-friendly products to ensure it is formulated (or reformulated) without marine-toxic ingredients or interaction, with superior sensorial attributes. This will ensure both efficacy and superior product experience that will encourage increase of reef-safe sunscreen.
We will also be proving clinical efficacy and toxicity effects of the chosen active form and excipients used in Magwai Reef-safe sunscreen and shampoo bars (uncoated inorganic UV filters). 
Finally, we will ensure that the formula we will develop is scalable and ready for commercial manufacturing and production.
